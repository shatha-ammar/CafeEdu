All About Espresso Machines

Your guide to mastering the art of espresso.

What is an Espresso Machine?

An espresso machine is a coffee-making device designed to force pressurized hot water through finely-ground coffee to produce a concentrated shot of espresso.

Key Features:

  • High-pressure brewing system.
  • Steam wand for milk frothing.
  • Variety of styles: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic.

Parts of an Espresso Machine

Most espresso machines have the following components:

  • Portafilter: Holds the ground coffee.
  • Boiler: Heats the water to the right temperature.
  • Group Head: Dispenses water through the coffee.
  • Steam Wand: Steams and froths milk for lattes and cappuccinos.
